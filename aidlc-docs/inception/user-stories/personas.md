# User Personas — NegotiateAI

---

## Persona 1: The Anxious Graduate (Alex)

- **Role**: Recent college graduate entering the job market
- **Age**: 22–25
- **Background**: First real job offer in hand, no prior negotiation experience
- **Goals**: Secure a higher starting salary without damaging the relationship with the employer; build confidence for future negotiations
- **Pain Points**: Doesn't know what to say, fears rejection, tends to hedge ("I was hoping maybe..."), unsure if their ask is reasonable
- **Negotiation Style**: Diplomatic or Collaborative
- **Primary Use Cases**: Salary negotiation strategy, roleplay practice against an AI employer, coaching on language softness

---

## Persona 2: The Budget-Conscious Renter (Jordan)

- **Role**: Student or young professional renting an apartment
- **Age**: 20–28
- **Background**: Facing a rent renewal increase, wants to push back but doesn't know how
- **Goals**: Reduce monthly rent or freeze the increase; understand what leverage they have
- **Pain Points**: Feels powerless against landlords, unsure what arguments are valid, worried about being evicted or penalized
- **Negotiation Style**: Assertive or Diplomatic
- **Primary Use Cases**: Rent negotiation scenario intake, strategy with data-backed scripts, roleplay against an AI landlord

---

## Persona 3: The Freelancer Leveling Up (Sam)

- **Role**: Junior-to-mid freelance developer or designer
- **Age**: 23–32
- **Background**: Has been working with a client at a low rate and wants to raise it
- **Goals**: Increase hourly/project rate without losing the client; frame the increase around delivered value
- **Pain Points**: Awkward about money conversations, doesn't know how to justify the raise, fears the client will walk
- **Negotiation Style**: Collaborative or Assertive
- **Primary Use Cases**: Freelance rate negotiation, value-framing scripts, roleplay against an AI client, coaching on confidence and framing

---

## Persona 4: The Repeat Practitioner (Morgan)

- **Role**: Any of the above, returning to practice multiple scenarios
- **Age**: Any
- **Background**: Has used NegotiateAI before and wants to practice different scenarios or styles
- **Goals**: Improve negotiation skills over time; try different styles; review coaching feedback
- **Pain Points**: Wants variety in AI pushback; wants to see improvement in coaching scores
- **Negotiation Style**: Any (may experiment with different styles)
- **Primary Use Cases**: All features; particularly interested in end-of-session coaching summary
